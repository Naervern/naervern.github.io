  • Comic strip/page

  • Social media stickers (512 x 512 resolution)

    • Regarding image resolution

    Final art resolution might be slightly bigger than the baseline. Example: if the baseline for 4Mpx is 2000 x 2000 resolution during the designing process the picture might be scaled to a larger resolution such as 2400 x 2400 or even bigger at no charge. The format Digital Painting resolution is to be expected as higher, around 10 Mpx and above.

    Regarding aspect ratio

    The picture can have any aspect ratio from 1:1 to 1:3 / 3:1 (larger dimension being three times the smaller dimension). For other aspect ratios such as 16:9 in 3820 x 2160 for use as ultra-HD wallpaper or 1080 x 2340 for use as a phone background or lock-screen, it should be discussed on a case-by-case basis yet the baselines are still valid for estimates.
